About Me

My name is Ron. I enjoy solving problems that can better the lives around me and connecting with people of different backgrounds and experiences in life. I'm someone who loves to learn about processes and how things work. I've grown up playing a few instruments, having bass as one of my favourite!

Being blessed to be able to travel around the world as i grew up, an important lesson i have learnt was if you would like to connect with people that are different, you can do so by learning their language or take part in their culture. I had the great opporunity to do both in my season at University, receiving my Bachelors of Management (UTS).

My spark for software development must of happened in high school, coding my first game in Visual basic. Right after I had the opportunity to work in the travel industry, learning amazing customer service, communication, being a contributing team member and primarily learning more about myself! Fast forward, now i'm in training to be a Full-stack developer @ Coder Academy , to build inspiring solutions!
